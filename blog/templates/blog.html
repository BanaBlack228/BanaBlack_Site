Forgotten Old

  • by Admin
  • 2 Comments

Um, I started the project by changing my nickname from "BlackBerry" to "BanaBlack".
The story is very interesting!.
It's the end of summer. I'm putting together a bunch of different tracks, and I decided to change my nickname and start posting proper music content.
I met with the already familiar you "@cactusvkadre" to discuss projects and future plans.
Then I approached a friend: "I want to change my nickname to something else, but I can't think of one. The idea is that I want two capital letters, BB, a nickname, and a prefix, like in my previous nickname."
Without thinking twice, my friend came up with a ridiculous idea: "Whatever the first word we see that starts with B, it will be!".
I agreed. We left the office and were just walking down the street when we came across an old garage covered in graffiti. And, damn it, a single word, and you won't believe it, it was the word with this letter!.
This is how my nickname came about!.
